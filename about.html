What about me?

I was born Toronto, but was raised in East Michigan. After I met my adoring wife, we lived in Colorado for a short time, only to trace our roots back home and settle Rochester Hills, Michigan. When not slinging code, I love to make music on guitars, drum kit, keyboards and in digital audio workstations like Reason, Pro Tools and Logic. I also like to read quite a lot about technology, nutrition and science. I'm also a big fan of the marvelous WordPress community of developers and designers that I am priviledged to be a part of.
